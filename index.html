Glacera Records concert

We at Glacera Records would like to invite you to a concert to celebrate.


Who's performing?

Tons of artists will be performing. For a full overview check out the schedule.

Some of your favorites include:

  • Lil Stump
  • Bitel Jaxon
  • The Meetles
  • SKISS
  • Javid Bower
  • The Buggies
  • Hard Cell
  • Stick Pastly

Where?

Harpan, Reykjavik, Iceland



When?

The concert is to be held at the 46th of January 3080.

The show starts at 19:00 with a warm-up while everybody gets in.


How old do I have to be?

The concert is for ages 12 and up. Anyone under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult.


What does it cost?

Tickets are 20,000kr. We also have discounts for large groups and kids 18 and under get in for free.
